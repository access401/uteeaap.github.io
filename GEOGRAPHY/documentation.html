About the Documentation

Documentation of the hardships involved is required. Whenever possible, content should be made accessible rather than provide alternates. If the content cannot be made accessible either because the technology to do so doesn't exist yet, or because the cost in time is too great, it must be documented. On this page are the guidelines for providing the required documentation for establishing the basis for determining that conformance to the requirements places an undue burden on the agency, or would result in a fundemental alteration of the Information Communications Technology (ICT)

E202.6.2 Required Documentation

The responsible agency official shall document in writing the basis for determining that conformance to requirements in the Revised 508 Standards constitute an undue burden on the agency, or would result in a fundamental alteration in the nature of the ICT. The documentation shall include an explanation of why and to what extent compliance with applicable requirements would create an undue burden or result in a fundamental alteration in the nature of the ICT.
