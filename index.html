Overview

The Equally Effective Alternate Access Plan (EEAAP) addresses the what, why, how, and when of making course content accessibe.

To the left are pages that provide background for all disciplines.

Discipline-Specific

Below are discipline-specific working aids. They generally contain four pages: a Home page, a Documentation page, a "General Solutions" page, and a "Resources" Page.

  • Home: Focus of Education
  • Documentation: Problems in making the discipline-specific files accessible
  • General Solutions: "Off-the-shelf" technhology that can be used, probably immediately, by those in the discipline
  • Resources: Examples of how others have solved the problem, and/or supplementary "how-to" guides.
